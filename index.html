How to Play

Listen to the order, then build the soup by choosing ingredients.

  1. Tap Hear Order to listen to the mystery chord.
  2. Tap ingredients to hear their sound and add them to the pot.
  3. Click the ladle to hear your soup.
  4. When you think it matches, tap Serve.
  5. Tap Next Order for a new one.

Use the Soup Recipe Board to see which ingredients belong in each recipe. Tap a recipe to mark it Sold Out if you do not want it tested.
